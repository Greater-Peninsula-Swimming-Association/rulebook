# Definitions

This section defines key terms used throughout the GPSA Rulebook.

## GPSA

Greater Peninsula Swimming Association.

## BONA FIDE MEMBER

An individual who meets all of the following criteria:

1. The individual shall be a member in good standing of the GPSA pool to be represented. For members representing a team composed of two or more pools, the individual need only be a member of one of the represented pools.

2. For competitive swimmers: Satisfies the age requirements for participation as specified in this rulebook.

3. Has a GPSA liability waiver signed by a legally appropriate representative, or by the individual themselves if of legal age.

4. An individual who is a member of a pool that has not sponsored a team for the season may represent another team for a period of 1 year.

## CHAMPIONSHIP MEET

1. Is a swim meet that is open to all qualifying swimmers from any GPSA **Team**.

2. Also referred to as CITY MEET.

## SUMMER SPLASH INVITATIONAL MEET

Is a swim meet that is open to swimmers not qualified for the Championship Meet.

## INTERPOOL MEET

Also known as a dual meet, shall mean a swim meet swum between two pools.

## TEAM

Is a recognized member of the GPSA that has access to a pool capable of hosting swimming competitions.

## DIVISION

1. A grouping of **Team**s who compete in GPSA-Scheduled swim meets in order to identify a DIVISION champion.

2. The GPSA operates three divisions, Red, White, and Blue.
