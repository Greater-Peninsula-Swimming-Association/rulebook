# Scoring

This section defines the point system for GPSA dual meets.

## Individual Events

1. For all individual events, five (5) points shall be awarded for first place, three (3) points for second place, and one (1) point for third place.

## Relay Events

1. The winning relay team shall be awarded seven (7) points, the losing team none.

    a. With exception for Event 0, this is a non-scored event.

## Ties

1. In case of ties, points for the tying and next position(s) will be added and divided equally between the tying swimmers.
